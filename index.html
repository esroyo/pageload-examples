Understanding page load

Loading a web site is a gradual process

Check it out with a page that has lots of content and takes long to be downloaded. Try to scroll down while the page is being downloaded. The display keeps updating, but DOMContentLoaded and load events won't be triggered until the download and parsing of the HTML is finished. There is no direct correlation between the delay on those events and a bad score on metrics like FCP, LCP, CLS or TBT.
